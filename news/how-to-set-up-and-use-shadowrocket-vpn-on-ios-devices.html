How to Set Up and Use Shadowrocket VPN on iOS Devices

How to Set Up and Use Shadowrocket VPN on iOS Devices

Posted by on 2024-09-14

In today's digital age, privacy and security have become paramount concerns for internet users. Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) offer a reliable solution for protecting one's online identity and data. Shadowrocket is one such VPN service that has garnered attention for its robust features and user-friendly interface. If you own an iOS device and are keen on enhancing your online security, setting up and using Shadowrocket VPN could be a game-changer. This essay will guide you through the process of installing and utilizing Shadowrocket on your iPhone or iPad.

 

The first step in this journey is to download the Shadowrocket app from the App Store. Open the App Store on your iOS device, search for "Shadowrocket," and proceed to download it. Given that it's a paid app, you'll need to purchase it before initiating the download. Once installed, locate the app icon on your home screen and tap to open it.

 

 

Upon launching Shadowrocket for the first time, you'll be greeted with a straightforward interface designed to facilitate easy navigation even for novice users. The main screen displays various options such as "Global Routing," "Rules," "Proxy," and more. Before diving into these settings, you need to configure a server that will act as your secure connection point.

 

 

To add a server, tap on the plus sign (+) located at the top right corner of the screen. This action will prompt you to enter details like server address, port number, username, password, encryption method, etc., depending on your VPN provider's requirements. It's crucial to ensure that these details are accurate; otherwise, you might face connectivity issues.

 

 

Once you've added all necessary information, save the configuration by tapping "Done." Your new server should now appear in the list of available servers. Select this server by tapping on it; this will mark it as your preferred connection point.

 

 

Next comes enabling the VPN connection itself. Simply toggle the switch located at the top of Shadowrocket’s main screen from “Off” to “On.” The app may prompt you to allow configuration changes or input your device passcode—grant these permissions when requested. Once activated successfully, an icon resembling a small key will appear at the top left corner of your screen indicating that you're connected via VPN.
